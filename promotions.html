No, only one bonus can be active at a time. You must complete the wagering requirements of your current bonus before claiming a new promotion. However, cashback bonuses run independently and can be claimed alongside other offers.

Slots contribute 100% to wagering requirements, while table games like blackjack and roulette contribute 10%. Live dealer games contribute 20%. Video poker and some specialty games may have different contribution rates detailed in the full terms.

What happens if I don't complete wagering within 30 days?
